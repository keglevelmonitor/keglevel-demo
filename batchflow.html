BatchFlow Help

Getting Started

BatchFlow is a brewing workflow board. Organize your batches across four stages:

  • Rotation — recipes you brew regularly
  • On Deck — next up to brew
  • Fermenting — currently in the fermenter
  • Finishing — conditioning, lagering, or carbonating

Adding Batches

Click + Add Batch at the top of any column to select a beverage from your library. If the beverage doesn't exist yet, click + New Beverage to create one.

Moving Batches

Drag a batch card from one column to another. Drop it between existing cards to control the order. You can also drag to the red Remove dock at the bottom to remove a batch from the board.

Columns

Click a column header to collapse or expand it. Double-click the header to rename the column.

Editing Beverages

Click the pencil icon on a batch card to edit the beverage details (name, ABV, IBU, SRM). Changes are saved to the Pico and reflected everywhere.
