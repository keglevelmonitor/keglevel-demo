KegLevel Pico

Know What's on Tap

A Raspberry Pi Pico-powered keg monitoring system that tracks pour volumes, keg levels, and temperature — all from your browser.

Features

  • Flow Monitoring — Hall-effect sensors track every pour in real time across up to 5 taps
  • Keg Levels — See remaining volume at a glance with visual level indicators
  • Temperature — Temperature sensor shows live kegerator temperature
  • Web Dashboard — Computer and Mobile-friendly web pages are served directly from the Pico — no app to install, no cloud needed
  • Web Dashboard Access — Access with any browser on your WiFi network. Or, skip your WiFi network and access with any device in Standalone mode — no WiFi needed. (Emails and OTA unavailable in Standalone mode)
  • BatchFlow — Built-in brewing workflow board to track batches from recipe to keg
  • Alerts — Email notifications available as regular reports or when a keg is low or the kegerator temperature is out-of-range
  • OTA Updates — Over-the-Air firmware updates via WiFi without disconnecting anything

What You Get

  • The KegLevel Pico System — wired, tested, and ready to install in your kegerator
  • Flow sensors — wired and tested — be sure to select the compatible beer line connection type for your kegerator
  • Installation and setup guide
  • Firmware updates — free of course

What Else You Might Need

  • To install the flow sensors in your beer lines, you may need a few short pieces of beverage tubing and some clamps
