Welcome to KegLevel Demo

This is a fully interactive demo of the KegLevel Brain keg monitoring system. No hardware required.

  • 5 taps pre-loaded with beverages and realistic keg levels
  • Live temperature readings that fluctuate naturally
  • Pour buttons to simulate dispensing and watch levels drop
  • Full inventory management — add, edit, and delete kegs and beverages
  • BatchFlow brewing workflow board with drag-and-drop
  • Some features (Alerts, Updates, WiFi) are view-only in demo mode
